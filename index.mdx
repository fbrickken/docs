## What You Can Do with the DApp
#### Issuers
Create and launch your own tokenized offerings, manage shareholders, and track investments – all from a simple, intuitive dashboard.

#### Investor
Browse offerings, complete KYC, purchase digital assets, and monitor your portfolio in real time.

#### On-chain and Off-chain Compliance
The DApp ensures a compliant framework by integrating identity verification, whitelisting, and transaction monitoring across jurisdictions.

#### Multi-network Support
Interact with assets deployed on various EVM-compatible blockchains, and manage operations across different networks effortlessly.


> With Brickken’s DApp, blockchain becomes a powerful tool – not a technical barrier. No coding required. Just a clean interface designed for real-world finance.
